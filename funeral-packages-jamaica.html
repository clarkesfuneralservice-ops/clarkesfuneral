Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest funeral package in Jamaica?

The most affordable option is Direct Cremation at $190,000. This includes all essential services: transfer, refrigeration, funeral director services, cremation, death registration, and ashes in a temporary container.

What's included in all funeral packages?

All packages include: transfer from place of death, refrigeration, funeral director services, death registration, certified death certificate(s), and either cremation or burial services. Higher-tier packages add viewing, services, programs, and additional features.

Do burial packages include the cemetery plot?

No, cemetery plots are purchased separately from the cemetery. Our burial packages ($310,000-$825,000) include the funeral service, casket, and burial assistance, but not the plot itself. We can help you arrange cemetery plot purchase.

Can I customize a funeral package?

Yes! While our packages offer great value, we can customize any package to meet your specific needs. You can add services like additional death certificates, upgraded urns or caskets, more programs, or remove services you don't need.

Do you offer payment plans for funeral packages?

Yes, we understand funeral costs can be challenging. We offer flexible payment plans to help families afford dignified services. Call 876-326-1263 to discuss payment options that work for your budget.

How do I choose between cremation and burial?

This is a personal decision based on religious beliefs, family preferences, and budget. Cremation is generally more affordable ($190K-$420K) while burial ranges from $310K-$825K plus cemetery plot. We're happy to discuss both options and help you decide.

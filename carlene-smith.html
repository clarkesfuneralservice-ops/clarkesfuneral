IN LOVING MEMORY

Carlene Leone Smith

"Ms. Smith"

June 28, 1971 – April 30, 2026

Age 54

It is with deep love and gratitude for her life that we announce the passing of our beloved Carlene Leone Smith, affectionately called "Ms. Smith", on April 30, 2026. She was a devoted mother, caring mother-in-law, loving grandmother, cherished sister and loyal friend to many.

She leaves to mourn her only child and daughter Shannel; son-in-law Jefferson (Jeff); and precious grandchildren Jerome and Dianxia.

She is also lovingly remembered by her brothers Junior, Hansel, Phillip, Patrick, Cornel, Caldeen, Leonard, and Daniel; her sisters Eileen, Lorna, and Alma; along with several nieces, nephews, other relatives, extended family and dear friends.

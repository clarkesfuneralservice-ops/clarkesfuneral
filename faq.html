Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about funeral services, cremation, burial, and memorial planning in Jamaica

üïäÔ∏è Cremation Services

Do you offer cremation services in Jamaica?
Yes. Clarke's Funeral Services provides comprehensive cremation services throughout Jamaica, including direct cremation, simple cremation, standard cremation, and premium cremation packages. We handle everything from transfer and refrigeration to the cremation process and delivery of ashes in your choice of urn.
Is cremation cheaper than burial in Jamaica?
Yes, cremation is typically more affordable than traditional burial. It eliminates costs such as cemetery plots, vaults, and permanent caskets. Our cremation packages start at $190,000 for direct cremation, while burial packages start at $455,000. However, the final cost depends on the specific services you choose.
How much does cremation cost in Kingston, Jamaica?
Our cremation packages range from $190,000 (direct cremation) to $440,000 (premium cremation with full service, live streaming, and urn vault). We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Visit our Pricing page for complete package details.
How long does the cremation process take?
The cremation process itself takes approximately 2-3 hours. However, the entire timeline from death to receiving ashes typically takes 5-7 days, depending on necessary authorizations, death certificate processing, and scheduling. We work efficiently to honor your loved one while respecting all legal requirements.
Can I have a viewing before cremation?
Absolutely. Our Simple, Standard, and Premium cremation packages include embalming and viewing options. Families can have a traditional visitation at our facility or a full funeral service before cremation. This allows loved ones to say goodbye and provides closure.

Do you provide burial services?
Yes. We offer complete traditional burial services including embalming, casket selection, hearse services, vault coordination, cemetery arrangements, and graveside services. Our burial packages range from $455,000 to $880,000 and include everything needed for a dignified farewell.
Which cemeteries do you work with?
We work with all major cemeteries throughout Jamaica, including Dovecot Memorial Park and other locations in Kingston, St. Andrew, and surrounding parishes. We coordinate directly with cemetery staff to ensure smooth arrangements and can assist with plot selection if needed.
What's included in your burial packages?
Our burial packages include: transfer of remains, refrigeration, embalming, washing and dressing, funeral service coordination, casket (local or imported hardwood), hearse service, funeral programs, prayer cards, casket piece (flowers), vault at cemetery, death registration, certified death certificates, and optional services like live streaming and death notices in media.

üìã General Services

Do you assist with death certificates?
Yes. We handle all death registration and secure certified copies of death certificates for your family. The number of certified copies included depends on your package (1-3 copies), and additional copies can be obtained if needed. This service is included in all our packages.
Do you provide international repatriation?
Yes. We specialize in international repatriation services, helping families transport loved ones to or from Jamaica. We handle all documentation, consulate coordination, embalming for transport, airline arrangements, and customs clearance. Our team has extensive experience with repatriation to the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, and other countries worldwide.
Do you offer memorial services?
Yes. We provide fully customized memorial services and celebration of life ceremonies. Our $308,000 memorial service package includes programs, prayer cards, live streaming, a framed photo, cremation, and urn. Memorial services can be held at churches, funeral homes, or other meaningful locations.
Do you provide live streaming for funerals?
Yes! Live streaming is included in our Standard and Premium packages (both cremation and burial). This allows family and friends around the world to participate in the service remotely. We handle all technical setup and provide a private link for viewing.
